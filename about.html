About Us

Captains of Tomorrow is designed to provide applicable and practical learning resources for high school students through the provision and development of seminars and hands-on programs. We strive to enhance diversified education to strengthen students' knowledge and passion in the field of business and law as a way to guide and prepare them for a better career path in the future.

Building Connections & Exposure to Experience and Opportunities

Students will gain exposure to the business and legal fields by learning how to pursue careers in these fields, what the day-to-day aspects of these relevant jobs look like, and by participating in a variety of enrichment activities. They will be able to gain practical skills and begin to pursue and learn about their areas of interest, gaining valuable knowledge and experience that are not taught in school. Moreover, they will be provided with opportunities to network and learn from esteemed professionals, test their skills against students from around the world, and network with like-minded individuals! They will be motivated to start moving forward and stand out as the next generation of youth leaders.
