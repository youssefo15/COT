What Do We Do

What Do We Do

Speak. Inspire, change “For the students, of the students, by the students.” Captains of Tomorrow is a student-led organization dedicated to molding today's young leaders into tomorrow's "Captains" by empowering high school students to learn and explore in the fields of business and law.

The Problem Statemnt

The problem

Surveys and studies show that more than 40 percent of students worldwide do not have access to business and law courses or even the opportunity to learn about them in school. In fact, many students are struggling to find their way beyond high school, and many of the options offered are limited to what is taught in school.

The Solution

The Solution

Hands-on resources and decryption topics COT strives to empower and educate the next generation of youth in the field of business and law, by doing so we connect like-minded youth from worldwide to expand knowledge, experience and skills that are not taught in school. Provide a comprehensive opportunity and platform for students to find their path, interest and passion in the field. We conduct informative and precise hands-on programs, seminars, conferences and forums that gives them the opportunity and chance to explore the world of business and law in a broader way and find the most suitable for them.
